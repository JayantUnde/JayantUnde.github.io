About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Nagoya University, specializing in Micro-Nano Mechanical Science and Engineering. My research is centered on developing innovative robotic systems, particularly for dressing assistance, and creating novel mechanisms like telescopic linear actuators. I am passionate about applying my expertise in mechanical design, robotics, and automation to solve real-world challenges.

I am currently completing my Ph.D. from Nagoya University. Previously, I worked as a Mechanical Design Engineer at Aker Solutions and held research internships at Polytechnique Montreal and MFTBC in Japan. I thrive in environments that challenge me to develop novel solutions and contribute to the fields of robotics and mechanical engineering.
